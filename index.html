My Philosophy

Hi, I'm Akash.

I am a Product & UX/UI Designer who sits at the intersection of business strategy, user psychology, and visual crafts. Over the past 5 years, I have worked with start-ups and tech teams to distill highly complex workflows into intuitive, clean interface models.

I believe design shouldn't just look pretty; it should solve real business metrics. I design systems with high fidelity, detailed motion curves, and clean architectures.
